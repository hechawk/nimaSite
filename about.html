My name is Nima and I am an illustrator based in Cologne, Germany. After receiving my bachelors in design at KISD (Köln International School of Design) I worked as and illustrator and graphic designer, mainly for TV and video games.
For the past year I mainly focused on the production of an handmade, animated sci- fi show called „Broke Boy$“ for which I do the art direction and production. Along with my brother and two friends I co-founded the production company World Wide Web Company and we received a government funding for the pilot episode.
Thanks for having a look!
